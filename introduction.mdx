## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="How long does setup take?">
They're deployed instantly! Our automatic system will activate your server immediately after placing an order.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What hardware do you use?">
Our servers use the most powerful hardware on the market, solely using NVMe SSDs and a minimum of 1Gbps network port for every server.

- CPU: **Ryzen 9 5950X** or better (Plus), **Ryzen 9 7950X** or better (Elder)
- Gen 3 and 4 NVMe SSDs with RAID 1 configuration
- **DDR4** RAM (Plus), **DDR5 ECC** RAM (Elder)
- **1Gbps** Network Port (Plus), **10Gbps** Network Port (Elder)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What locations do you offer?">
**Dallas, Texas** (Central USA), **Ashburn, Virginia** (East USA), **London, United Kingdom** (EU), **Germany** (EU), and **Amsterdam** (EU).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
We support **PayPal**, **Credit/Debit Card** (powered by [Stripe](https://stripe.com)), **SEPA** (powered by [Stripe](https://stripe.com)), and Cryptocurrency (powered by [Coinbase Commerce](https://www.coinbase.com/commerce)).
</Accordion>
