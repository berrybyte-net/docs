# Cancel Your Subscription

Cancel your server anytime. No hidden fees.

## How to Cancel

### 1. Save Your Data (Optional)

Download any backups you want to keep. Once cancelled, your server data will be deleted.

### 2. Go to Billing

Log in to [billing.berrybyte.net](https://billing.berrybyte.net/login) and select your server.

### 3. Request Cancellation

Click **Request Cancellation**, choose **Immediate** or **End of Billing Period**, and submit.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/general/cancellation.png" />
</Frame>

Cancellations are processed at 9AM CST daily. Once processed, all server data is removed.

## Having Issues?

**Can't find the cancellation button?**
- Make sure you're logged into the right account
- [Contact support](https://berrybyte.net/contact) if you still can't find it

**Want to keep your data?**
- [Create a backup](/panel/backups) before cancelling
- Download the backup to your computer

**Cancelled by mistake?**
- [Contact us](https://berrybyte.net/contact) right away
- We can reactivate if the server hasn't been deleted yet
