You can cancel your server anytime. No hidden fees or lock-ins.

---

## Before You Cancel (Optional)

If you want to keep your world, saves, or files:

- Create a backup  
- Download the backup to your computer  

Once your subscription is cancelled and processed, **all server data is permanently deleted**.

---

## How to Cancel

### 1. Log in to Billing

Go to **[billing.berrybyte.net](https://billing.berrybyte.net/login)** and select the service you want to cancel.

### 2. Request Cancellation

Click **Request Cancellation** and choose:

- **Immediate** — deletes the server as soon as cancellation is processed  
- **End of Billing Period** — your server stays online until your next renewal date

<Info>
  Cancellations are processed daily at **9AM CST**.  
  After processing, all server data is removed and cannot be recovered.
</Info>

## Having Issues?

### Can't find the cancellation button?
- Double-check you're logged into the correct billing account  
- Still stuck? [Contact support](https://berrybyte.net/contact)

### Want to keep your data?
- Make a backup before cancelling  
- Download the backup to your computer for safekeeping  

### Cancelled by mistake?
- [Contact us immediately](https://berrybyte.net/contact)  
- If the server hasn’t been deleted yet, we can reactivate it
