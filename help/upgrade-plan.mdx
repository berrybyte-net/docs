# Upgrade Your Server Plan

Need more RAM, players, or performance? Upgrade your plan anytime. Your files stay safe and upgrades happen instantly.

## How to Upgrade

### 1. Go to Billing

Log in to [billing.berrybyte.net](https://billing.berrybyte.net/login) and select your server.

Click **Upgrade/Downgrade** in the left menu.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/general/upgrade-01.png" />
</Frame>

### 2. Choose Your New Plan

Pick the plan you want. You'll only pay the difference for the remaining days in your billing cycle.

### 3. Restart Your Server

Restart your server from the dashboard to apply the new resources.

## Having Issues?

**Can't see upgrade options?**
- Make sure you're logged into the right account
- You might already be on the highest plan
- [Contact support](https://berrybyte.net/contact) for help

**Resources not working after upgrade?**
- Restart your server
- Wait 5-10 minutes
- Still not working? [Get help](https://berrybyte.net/contact)
