# Best Satisfactory Mods

Essential mods to enhance your factory building experience on your [Satisfactory server](https://berrybyte.net/satisfactory-server-hosting). All mods are available through [Satisfactory Mod Manager (SMM)](/games/satisfactory/install-mods).

## Quality of Life Mods

### [Smart Foundations](https://ficsit.app/mod/SmartFoundations)
Advanced foundation placement with auto-alignment, grid snapping, and mass placement tools.

### [Area Actions](https://ficsit.app/mod/AreaActions)
Mass building operations with delete, copy/paste, and area selection tools.

### [Pak Utility Mod](https://ficsit.app/mod/PakUtilityMod)
Collection of building utilities including copy tools and measurement helpers.

## Building Enhancement Mods

### [Refined Power](https://ficsit.app/mod/RefinedPower)
Advanced power systems with solar panels, wind turbines, and monitoring tools.

### [Structural Solutions](https://ficsit.app/mod/StructuralSolutions)
Additional building pieces including new walls, roofs, and decorative elements.

### [Conveyor Wall Hole](https://ficsit.app/mod/ConveyorWallHole)
Clean conveyor belt routing through walls with multiple hole sizes.

## Production Optimization Mods

### [Factory Statistics](https://ficsit.app/mod/FactoryStatistics)
Production monitoring with rates, efficiency metrics, and bottleneck detection.

### [Resource Sink Shop Plus](https://ficsit.app/mod/ResourceSinkShopPlus)
Enhanced awesome shop with bulk purchasing and search functionality.

### [Production Planner](https://ficsit.app/mod/ProductionPlanner)
Factory planning tools with production calculators and resource optimization.

## Visual and Interface Mods

### [Better Concrete](https://ficsit.app/mod/BetterConcrete)
Enhanced concrete textures with higher resolution and color variants.

### [Factory Lighting Plus](https://ficsit.app/mod/FactoryLightingPlus)
Advanced lighting with LED strips, flood lights, and automated systems.

### [UI Improvements](https://ficsit.app/mod/UIImprovements)
Better interface with improved inventory management and cleaner menus.

## Logistics and Transportation Mods

### [Conveyor Improvements](https://ficsit.app/mod/ConveyorImprovements)
Belt counters, flow indicators, and throughput displays for optimization.

### [Teleporter Mod](https://ficsit.app/mod/TeleporterMod)
Fast travel system with teleporter pads for large factory navigation.

### [Vehicle Improvements](https://ficsit.app/mod/VehicleImprovements)
Enhanced vehicle AI with automated routes and cargo management.

## Installation Tips

**Essential Combinations:**
- Beginner: Smart Foundations + Area Actions + Pak Utility Mod
- Production: Factory Statistics + Production Planner + Refined Power
- Building: Better Concrete + Factory Lighting Plus + Structural Solutions

**Best Practices:**
- Add mods gradually and monitor performance
- Check dependencies before installing
- Backup saves before adding new mods
- Keep mods updated regularly

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="How many mods can I install safely?">
Most players can run 10-15 mods without issues. Add gradually and monitor performance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do mods affect save compatibility?">
Some mods may make saves incompatible with vanilla game. Always backup saves first.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use mods on servers?">
Yes, but all players must have identical mods. Server admins control required mod lists.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I update mods safely?">
Use SMM's update feature and backup saves first. Test updates before multiplayer use.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Multiplayer mod synchronization issues?">
Ensure all players have identical mod lists and versions. Use SMM profiles for consistency.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
