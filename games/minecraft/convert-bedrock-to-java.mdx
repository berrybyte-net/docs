Convert your Minecraft Bedrock worlds to Java using [Chunker](https://chunker.app/), a tool developed by the HiveMC team.

Note: Conversion isn't perfect due to differences between the two editions.

## Export Your Bedrock World

1. Open Minecraft Bedrock Edition
2. Click **Play**
3. Find the world you want to convert and click the edit button (pencil icon)
4. Click **Export World** at the bottom of the Game Settings page
5. Save the `.mcworld` file to your computer 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/games/minecraft/export-world-bedrock.png" alt="Exporting a Bedrock World from Minecraft Bedrock Edition"/>
</Frame>

## Converting the World

1. Go to [chunker.app](https://chunker.app/)
2. Click **Upload archive**
3. Select your `.mcworld` file
4. Click **Start Upload**
5. Select the Java Edition version you want (keep it consistent with your server)
6. Click **Convert & Download** and wait
7. Click **Download** when done

Download links are valid for **30 minutes**.

Once downloaded, copy the world folder into your Java Edition saves folder.
